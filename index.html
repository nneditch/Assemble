What is Assemble?

Assemble is a web and mobile application where you can connect to actions and opportunities from mission-driven organizations.

Personalized

Actions are curated for you based on personal interests, location, and skills.

Smart

Your actions will evolve over time based on what moves you.

Rewarding

Earn points and rewards as you do good. See the impact of your efforts.

Secure

Rest assured that we take your personal privacy and security seriously.

Are you a mission-driven organization looking to drive new support to your campaigns?

Partner organizations get access to new volunteers, as well as data on the issues people care about and the actions they are most likely to take.
